3D Humane Eye Simulator

Welcome to the Interactive 3D Humane Eye Simulator

Description

This project simulates the human eye, focusing on key components like the cornea and lens, with approximated curvatures, to demonstrate how light behaves as it passes through the eye. It includes simulations of common vision pathologies such as myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, and spherical aberrations, illustrating their impact on the light's path through the eye
